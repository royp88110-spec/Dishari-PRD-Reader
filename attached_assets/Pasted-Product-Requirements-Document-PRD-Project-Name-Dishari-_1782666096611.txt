Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project Name
Dishari mess – Meal Counter & Expense Management System

1. Project Overview
Dishari mess  a application designed to automate meal tracking, expense management, advance payments, and monthly settlements for shared messes, hostels, and student accommodations.
The system eliminates manual calculations and ledger maintenance by providing real-time meal counts, expense tracking, and transparent member billing.

2. Objectives
	•	Automate daily meal tracking
	•	Reduce manual calculation errors
	•	Simplify expense management
	•	Generate accurate monthly bills
	•	Track member advances and dues
	•	Provide transparent financial reporting
	•	Support Bengali and English languages

Egg Tracking Management
Daily Egg Entry
Admin can manually record egg consumption:

	•	Select Member Name
	•	Enter Number of Eggs Consumed
	•	Save Record
Egg Price Management
	•	Admin manually enters the current Egg Price.
	•	Egg Price can be updated anytime.
	•	Price changes apply to future calculations.
Monthly Egg Summary
System automatically calculates:
	•	Total Eggs Consumed
	•	Current Egg Price
	•	Total Egg Cost
Egg Cost Calculation
Total Egg Cost = Total Eggs Consumed × Egg Price
Member-wise Egg Bill
Member Egg Bill = Member Total Eggs Consumed × Egg Price
Final Settlement
Member Egg Bill is added separately to the member's monthly bill.

Monthly Calculation Engine
Total Monthly Expense
Total Monthly Expense = Grocery + Vegetable Expenses + Fish + Meat + Gas + Cook Salary + Other Expenses
Total Monthly Meals
Total Monthly Meals = Total Morning Meals + Total Night Meals
Per Meal Cost
Per Meal Cost = Total Monthly Expense ÷ Total Monthly Meals
Member Gross Bill
Member Gross Bill = (Member Total Meals × Per Meal Cost) + Member Egg Bill
Member Egg Bill
Member Egg Bill = Member Total Eggs Consumed × Egg Price
Final Settlement
Final Due Amount = (Member Gross Bill + Member Egg Bill) − Member Advance Payment
Credit Balance Rule
If Advance Payment > (Member Gross Bill + Member Egg Bill):
Credit Balance = Advance Payment − (Member Gross Bill + Member Egg Bill)
Final Due Amount = 0
Note:
	•	Egg expenses are calculated separately from meal expenses.
	•	Advance payments are not expenses and do not affect the per-meal cost calculation.
Fixed Cook Salary Module
	•	Cook salary is charged as a fixed amount per member.
